Everyone (both human and pandas) loves pokemon a lot. It is always interesting to have pets you can save in a poke-balls in your pocket. Guo loves pokemon too, he want to have his own pokemon.

A newbie pokemon trainer like Guo need to choose his initial pokemon based on his favorite color. The pokemon center has already send them a rule to determine anyone's initial pokemon. However, Guo's friends doesn't understand the rule. They prefer if there is a program that can decide their initial pokemon, and they only need to type their favorite color.

  • They who like Red will get Charmender
  • They who like Green will get Bulbasur
  • They who like Blue will get Squirtle
  • They who like Black, White, or any other color will get Snorlax

You see? there are branch here. The output of the program is determined by it's input. Help Guo and his friends of Pandaria to overcome the problem.

Input :
Blue

Output :
Squirtle
